Lets know more about Dead sea

The Dead Sea boasts an impressive location, bordering both Palestine and Jordan. It spans 67 kilometers long and up to 18 kilometers wide along the Jordan Rift Valley. Both Palestine and Jordan boast many beautiful beaches that line the Dead Sea.
