Lets know more about Umm Qais

It is the largest city in the Bani Kinanah Department and Irbid Governorate in the extreme northwest of the country, near Jordan's borders with Palestine and Syria. Today, the site is divided into three main areas: the archaeological site (Gadara), the traditional village (Umm Qais), and the modern town of Umm Qais

Attractions in Umm Qais

  2. Roman Tombs
  3. The Basilica Terrace
  4. Ottoman Village
